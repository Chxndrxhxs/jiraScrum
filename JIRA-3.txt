3. What is a Sprint in Scrum, and how does the Sprint Timeline help the development team?

Answer-:
----------

A Sprint is a fixed time period, usually 1 to 4 weeks, during which the development team works to complete a set of selected User Stories. It is the core unit of work in Scrum methodology.

How Sprint Timeline helps:

->It gives a visual representation of tasks with start and end dates
->It helps identify dependencies between tasks
->It shows the workload of each team member
->It helps detect delays early so the team can take corrective action before the sprint ends
