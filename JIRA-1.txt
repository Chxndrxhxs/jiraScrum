1. What is the purpose of creating a project in JIRA, and what are the basic steps involved in project creation?

Answer-:
--------

A project in JIRA is created to organize and manage all the tasks, issues, and workflows of a team in one centralized place. It helps teams track progress, assign responsibilities, and collaborate effectively.

Basic Steps:

->Log in to JIRA and click "Create Project"
->Select a project type (Scrum or Kanban)
->Enter the project name and project key
->Add Epics, User story and a Backlog
->Configure the board and workflow settings
->Start the Sprint
